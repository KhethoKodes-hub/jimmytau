About Jimmy Tau Investments

Pioneering socio-economic development in South Africa since 2011

Our Collaborative Footprint

Since 2011

Our collaborative footprint in the enrichment of societal and economical development surpasses what a single entity can achieve. Our core purpose is to foster the creation of interactive relationships between all entities to maximize on social and economic growth.

Since our emergence into the investments realm in 2011, our vision, core values and mission has governed us to the success we have achieved.

Our Vision & Mission

Guiding principles that shape our investment decisions and partnerships.

Vision

To be an integral part, in the social and economic development of South African communities.

Mission

Jimmy Tau Investment's mission is to create transformative partnerships that uplift communities and drive sustainable growth.

Stimulate Interaction

Stimulate interaction between the state and innovative entrepreneurs to uplift all South African communities.

Add Value to Government

Add value to South African governmental structures through strategic partnerships and innovative solutions.

Economic Growth Participation

To be a part of economic growth investment opportunities that create sustainable value.

Quality Service Delivery

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every partnership and investment decision.

Innovation

Continuously improve our internal skills and structures and within our partnerships, to ensure quality service is delivered.

Ethics

Oppose any form of corruption. Maintain respectfulness towards all stakeholders in every interaction.

Commitment

  • Continue to participate in business that adds value to the community
  • Maximizing on any opportunities of personal and business growth

Communication
